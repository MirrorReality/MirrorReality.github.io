Our history

Mirror Reality JQ was formed in 2014 in Rostov-on-Don, Russia. The crew consisted of Ilya Blazh (Drums), Yana Torosyan (Bass Guitar), Artem Badenko (Tenor Saxophone, EWI) the music scholars of Kim Nazaretov Children's Jazz School and Mehak Torosyan (Piano) the student of Rostov State Rachmaninov Conservatoire. And all of them were close friends for many years. Meeting on different stages and projects they always seem to have something special when they played together, sharing a passion for jazz. In summer 2014 they had an idea of organizing open-air jam-sessions, so that rostovites could enjoy contemporary jazz and musicians original compositions. Inspired by warm encouragement from audience they decided to promote their project.

The "Festival of Good Music" in Rostov-on-Don became a great opportunity for musicians to announce the new group Mirror Reality JQ. Demo-video clip recorded for the Festival was approved by the managers and jury. The band was added to the schedule of the event. Inspired by success they had, jazz quartet decided to record a CD of their own music named "Refraction".

In May 2015 Yana and Mehak Torosyan left the group and their place took two gifted young jazz musicians Mike Voeykov (Bass Guitar) and Vlad Danilin (Piano) also the music scholars of Kim Nazaretov Children's Jazz School.

In summer 2015 they recorded a new album "Catharsis". The disk is composed entirely of original pieces with perfect rhythm and touch of free improvisation.

In spite of their young age every musician in quartet is already a mature-sounding artist. By present day they has participated in many international competitions and festivals in Russia, Argentina, Switzerland, Germany, Turkey, Serbia, Israel, Ukraine and Belorus.
